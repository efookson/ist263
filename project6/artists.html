Tame Impala

Australian artist Kevin Parker, writes, records, performs, and produces all his musical creations. He creates psychedelic pop/rock music starting his career with Tame Impala EP and after writing four studio albums with his most recent being The Slow Rush. Now he is signed with Interscope records in the United States as well as Fiction Records in the United Kingdom. He has received many awards for his music including APRA Award for Song of the Year for his Current track called “Let It Happen”. What is so unique about Tame Impala is the musical style and decisions he makes with the music. Constructing symphonies of sounds that come together to create an experience for listeners. While playing live they tend to change their songs timing, slowing it down/speeding it up. Experimentation is how they have come to make some of their most famous new- sounding songs.

His most famous songs are “The Less I Know the Better” Currents, “Feels Like We Only Go Backwards” Lonerism, “Elephant” Lonerism, “Let It Happen” Currents, and “Borderline” The Slow Rush.

Singer songwriter Dominic Fike creates alternative pop rock music receiving his first recognition after posting his music onto a popular website called SoundCloud. He began to record his music in his bedroom then went to sign with Columbia Records after a war broke out with multiple recording labels. His song “3 Nights” reached the top 10 in multiple countries which is on his first EP called Don't Forget About Me. His fame blew up and now he has a song in the movie Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse called “Mona Lisa”, including two studio albums called What Could Possibly Go Wrong and Sunburn. Being such a popular new artist in 2021, he recorded a cover of Paul McCartney's song “The Kiss of Venus” for the album McCartney III Imagines. Following his music career, he has also picked up acting and is a main character in the HBO series Euphoria.

His most popular songs are “Mona Lisa” Sunburn, “Babydoll” Don't Forget About Me, EP, “3 Nights” Don't Forget About Me, EP, “Phone Numbers”, and “Why” What Could Possibly Go Wrong.

The Driver Era

This duo consisting of brothers Rocky and Ross Lynch are alternative rock and R&B artists. Ross Lynch got his fame from Disney Channel after being on a popular TV show called “Austin and Ally”. This led to his band R5 to start playing songs on Disney Channel to eventually grow into their own identity. Now former members of the band R5, they have created three studio albums including X, Girlfriend, and Summer Mixtape. The name of their band came about in a conversation between Ross and Rocky in a car ride and they agreed the name described where they were headed in their music career.

There most popular songs are “A Kiss” Girlfriend, “Afterglow” X, “Preacher Man” X, “Low” X, and “Heavan Angel” Girlfriend.

Remi Wolf is a funk pop artist who self-started her career with her album You're A Dog! in October 2019. Before this album she appeared on American Idol in 2014 to try to jump start her career however she was not able to make it past the audition stage. Once graduating from USC Thornton School of Music in 2018, she was able to focus more on her career releasing songs with more famous people and even opening for Still Woozy on tour. After this she was able to create many singles leading her to sign with Island Records and develop her most famous song “Photo ID”. It was then remixed with Dominic Fike which was able to give her more fame. Her first album “Juno” is now her latest creation and is an artist that is known for her unique and funky music style.

Her most famous songs are “Photo ID” I'm Allergic To Dogs!, “Disco Man” I'm Allergic To Dogs!, “Sexy Villain” Juno, “Liz”, and “Cheesin” with Cautious Clay, sophie meiers, Still Woozy, Claud, Melanie Faye, and HXNS.

Wallows

Wallows is an alternative rock band that is based in Los Angeles. The three members of this band are Dylan Minnette, Braeden Lemasters, and Cole Preston. In 2017, their first Spotify Global Viral 50 chart song was a single called “Pleaser” which brought the band recognition globally. Once this song went viral they had sold out shows in the Los Angelas area playing live at huge theaters. They then signed on with Atlantic Records to release their major label debut called Spring. In 2019 the band released their biggest song, yet which was “Are You Bored Yet?” with another artist called Clario. This single ended up becoming a 2x certified platinum single being the band's first, while also reaching 8 and 13 on the Billboard Alternative and Rock Album charts.

Their most popular songs are “Are You Bored Yet? (feat. Clario)” Nothing Happens, “Remember When” Nothing Happens, “Pleaser” Spring, “Scrawny” Nothing Happens, and “These Days” Spring.

The Weeknd

Mixing together R&B, pop, and hip-hop to develop his music, The Weeknd tells stories with his songs. Multi-platinum 3x Grammy award winning artist has become world famous. He is the first artist to win “Best Urban Contemporary Album” twice in a musical career. What got him all these awards was his unconventional production and his performing ideas. Worldwide The Weekend has sold over 75 million records and is among one of the world's best-selling musical artists. The Weeknd performed at the Super Bowl halftime show in 2021 and also has created multiple songs for movies and television such as Blank Panther and Fifty Shades of Grey.

His most famous songs are “Die For You” Starboy, “Starboy” Starboy, “Blinding Lights” After Hours, “Save Your Tears” After Hours and these are naming a few since The Weeknd has been on the global charts many times.

ABBA is a Swedish pop group who gained fame after going on Eurovision with their song “Waterloo”. Group members Agnetha Faltskog, Bjorn Ulvaeus, Benny Andersson, and Anni-Frid Lyngstad made the name of the band using the first letters of their names. In 1999, ABBA's music was adapted into a stage musical called Mamma Mia! This musical became one of the longest running productions on Broadway and still brings them fame from the younger generations. Mamma Mia! developed eventually into a movie and a sequel using all their songs to develop the musical numbers. One of their most famous songs “Dancing Queen” is in the Recording Academy's Grammy Hall of Fame. In the United Kingdom, they had eight consecutive number one albums which allowed them to become one of the best-selling Swedish bands of all time.

Their most famous songs are “Dancing Queen” Arrival, “Gimme! Gimme! Gimme!” Voulez-Vous, “Mamma Mia” Abba, “Lay All Your Love On Me” Super Trouper, and “Money, Money, Money” Arrival.

Pitbull

Pitbull is known for his pop-rap music style, and he is also known as “Mr. 305” or “Mr. Worldwide”. He has sold over 25 million albums, over 100 million singles globally and was ranked one of the top artists of the 2010s. He created the song for the 2014 FIFA World Cup along with Jennifr Lopez and Claudia Leitte called “We Are One (Ole Ola)”. Pitbull also won 35 Billboard Latin Music Awards overall for his achievements in the Latin music industry. To get these achievements, he started producing music through Luther Campbell Records and Pitbull was introduced to people signed along with the record label so they can develop songs together. Lil Jon was one of the artists he was introduced to and the tracks they made together, made him become known. They made the song “Culo” which eventually peaked at number 32 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

His most famous songs are “Time of Our Lives” Globalization, “Timber (feat. Ke$ha)” Global Warming, “Hotel Room Service” Pitbull Starring In Rebelution, “Give Me Everything (feat. Ne-Yo, Afrojack & Nayer)” Planet Pit, and “Feel This Moment (feat. Christina Aguilera)” Global Warming.

This superstar has not only done music but also has acted along with being a businesswoman for her makeup company Fenty. She has 9 Grammy awards and recently performed during the Super Bown Halftime Show in 2023, which was the most watched in history.

Her most famous songs are “We Found Love” Talk That Talk, “Diamonds” Unapologetic, “Umbrella” Good Girl Gone Bad, “Only Girl(In The World)” Loud, “S&M” Loud, and “Don't Stop The Music” Good Girl Gone Bad.
